System Capabilities

Fixing the flaws of traditional, paper-based municipalities.

Geo-Tagged Issue Routing

Citizens pinpoint issues like potholes or water leaks with sub-meter accuracy via Live Maps. The system automatically routes the ticket to the specific Ward Department responsible for resolution, completely eliminating the "wrong desk" problem.

Smart Sifarish

End the long lines at the ward office. Digitize the application, tracking, and signing of official recommendation letters.

AI Guidance Chatbot

Navigate complex civic processes instantly. Our AI provides immediate answers to municipal regulations in English & Nepali.

Two-Way Transparency Dashboard

Unlike existing systems which focus solely on documents, CivicSewa creates a two-way street. Citizens track municipal response times live, and Ward Officials get a real-time analytics heat map of local emergencies and broadcasts.
